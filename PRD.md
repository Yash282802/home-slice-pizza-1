# Product Requirements Document (PRD) for Home Slice Pizza Website
## Overview
The goal of this project is to design and develop a website for Home Slice Pizza, a pizza restaurant business. The website should provide an online presence for the business, allowing customers to learn about the restaurant, view menus, place orders, and make reservations.

## Business Goals
The primary business goals for the Home Slice Pizza website are:
* Increase online orders and sales
* Improve customer engagement and loyalty
* Enhance brand awareness and reputation
* Provide a convenient and user-friendly platform for customers to interact with the restaurant

## Target Audience
The target audience for the Home Slice Pizza website includes:
* Demographics: Foodies, families, young professionals, and individuals aged 18-55
* Psychographics: People who value convenience, quality, and variety in their dining experiences
* Location: Primary target area is the local community, with potential for expansion to surrounding areas

## Pages Needed
The following pages are required for the Home Slice Pizza website:
* **Home**: Introduction to the restaurant, featuring promotions, news, and calls-to-action
* **Menu**: Detailed menu with descriptions, prices, and images of dishes
* **Order Online**: Online ordering system for customers to place orders for pickup or delivery
* **Reservations**: System for customers to make reservations for dine-in
* **About**: Information about the restaurant's history, mission, and values
* **Contact**: Contact information, including phone number, email, and physical address
* **Blog**: Optional page for posting news, recipes, and other engaging content

## Features
The following features are required for the Home Slice Pizza website:
* **Responsive Design**: Website should be optimized for viewing on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
* **Online Ordering System**: Integration with a third-party online ordering system or development of a custom system
* **Payment Gateway**: Secure payment processing for online orders
* **Reservation System**: Integration with a third-party reservation system or development of a custom system
* **Menu Management**: Admin panel for easy menu updates and management
* **Promotions and Discounts**: System for creating and managing promotions, discounts, and loyalty programs
* **Social Media Integration**: Links to social media profiles and potential integration with social media APIs
* **Customer Reviews**: System for customers to leave reviews and ratings
* **Newsletter Signup**: Optional feature for customers to sign up for newsletters and promotions

## Success Metrics
The success of the Home Slice Pizza website will be measured by the following key performance indicators (KPIs):
* **Website Traffic**: Number of unique visitors, page views, and bounce rate
* **Online Orders**: Number of online orders, revenue generated, and conversion rate
* **Reservation Bookings**: Number of reservations, revenue generated, and conversion rate
* **Customer Engagement**: Social media engagement, customer reviews, and newsletter signup rate
* **Customer Retention**: Repeat business, customer loyalty, and retention rate

## Technical Requirements
The website should be built using modern web development technologies, including:
* **Front-end**: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and a front-end framework (e.g. React, Angular)
* **Back-end**: Server-side programming language (e.g. PHP, Python, Ruby) and a content management system (e.g. WordPress, Drupal)
* **Database**: Relational database management system (e.g. MySQL, PostgreSQL)
* **Hosting**: Reliable web hosting service with adequate storage, bandwidth, and security features

## Timeline and Milestones
The project timeline will be approximately 12 weeks, with the following milestones:
* **Week 1-2**: Project planning, requirements gathering, and wireframing
* **Week 3-6**: Front-end development, back-end development, and database integration
* **Week 7-10**: Testing, debugging, and quality assurance
* **Week 11-12**: Launch preparation, deployment, and post-launch review

## Budget and Resources
The budget for the project will be approximately $15,000, broken down into:
* **Development**: $10,000
* **Design**: $2,000
* **Project Management**: $1,000
* **Testing and QA**: $1,000
* **Miscellaneous**: $1,000

The project will require a team of 2-3 people, including a project manager, front-end developer, back-end developer, and designer.
